Some interesting facts on rubies. Belive it or not, rubies are one of the most expensive gemstones on the market. They value in corolation to there rarity and purity. Rubies are consistantly made of three oxygen atoms and two aluminum atoms. The word ruby comes from the Latin rubens, which means red. And in actuality, a ruby is a pink to blood-red colored gemstone not purly red. And although rubies are said to be found all around the world, the reason they are marketed at a high value is becuase they are found in small quantities.
